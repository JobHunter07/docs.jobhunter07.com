# Preface

**Product Requirements Document (PRD) — JobHunter07**

This PRD defines the vision, mission, and MVP scope for JobHunter07. The language below is concise and action-oriented, aligned with the JobHunter07 brand voice: tactical, calm, and focused.

## Vision & Mission

**Vision.** Transform the job search into a hunter-driven career engine: developers pursue opportunities with precision, confidence, and strategic intent.

**Mission.** Empower developers to take control of their careers through proactive hunting by providing tools, intelligence, and workflows that surface hidden opportunities and support intentional outreach.

### Key principles

- Shift mindset from searching to hunting: adopt agency and momentum.
- Restore career control: let users identify companies, map decision-makers, and track outreach.
- Modernize hiring: prioritize human connection and skills over keyword tricks.
- Build community: shared insights and mentorship accelerate progress.

### Initial focus (MVP scope)

- Profession: .NET / C# developers
- Region: United States
- Language: English
- Work type: Remote-first

## Strategic Pillars

1. **Proactive Intelligence** — Surface companies, people, and signals before roles become public.
2. **Empowered Action** — Workflows for tracked outreach and repeatable follow-ups.
3. **Community Strength** — Shared insights and peer support.
4. **Human-Centered Technology** — Clear, fair systems that amplify real skills.

## Product Overview

JobHunter07 is a proactive job-hunting platform that blends AI, data, and human workflows to surface hidden opportunities, match skills to roles, and manage outreach end-to-end.

### Problems addressed

- Many roles never post publicly (~70%).
- Job boards produce noise and low signal.
- ATS and filters block qualified candidates.

## Goals & Objectives

**Business Goals**

- Connect job seekers to hidden opportunities and increase placements.
- Establish JobHunter07 as the leader in proactive job discovery.

**Product Objectives**

- Deliver an MVP focused on hidden-opportunity detection and matching.
- Ship a mobile-first, accessible UI (WCAG 2.1 AA).
- Integrate with major job boards and ATS.

## Target Users & Market Segments

**Primary users:**

- Active job seekers
- Passive candidates open to better opportunities
- Early-career developers and those with limited networks

**Employers & partners:**

- Recruiters, HR teams, and career coaches

## Core MVP Features

- Intelligent Job Discovery Engine (AI/ML for hidden roles)
- User Profile Builder (skills-first profiles)
- Matching Algorithm (skill-based, semantic)
- Smart Alerts & Notifications
- Application Tracker and Outreach Workflows
- Analytics Dashboard for users and employers

## Success Metrics (examples)

- DAU/MAU, activation rate, and conversion to interviews
- Match relevance and résumé parsing accuracy
- Employer retention and revenue metrics
