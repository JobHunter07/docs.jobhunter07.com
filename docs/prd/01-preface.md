## Product Overview

JobHunter07 is a proactive job-hunting platform that blends AI, data, and human workflows to surface hidden opportunities, match skills to roles, and manage outreach end-to-end.

### Problems addressed

<Warning>
**Critical market gaps:**
- Many roles never post publicly (~70%)
- Job boards produce noise and low signal
- ATS and filters block qualified candidates
</Warning>

## Matching Algorithms

### Algorithmic Approaches

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Linear & Logistic Regression" icon="chart-line">
    Initial suitability scoring and binary match/no-match decisions
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Decision Trees & Random Forests" icon="tree">
    Multi-criteria matching, skill assessment, and candidate categorization
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Support Vector Machines (SVM)" icon="vector-square">
    Advanced pattern recognition and personalized matching
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Gradient Boosting Machines (GBM)" icon="rocket">
    Ranking candidates and jobs, handling complex profiles
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Natural Language Processing (NLP)" icon="language">
    Résumé parsing, job description analysis, semantic search, and skill extraction
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Feedback Loops" icon="arrows-rotate">
    Continuous learning from user interactions to refine match accuracy
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Bias Detection & Fairness" icon="scale-balanced">
    Regular audits to identify and mitigate algorithmic bias
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
**Algorithm Selection Rationale:** Linear and tree-based models are prioritized for the MVP because they offer transparency, interpretability, and strong scalability with limited data. As platform usage grows, more advanced ensemble and deep-learning models can be introduced.
</Tip>

## Testing Strategy

<Steps>
  <Step title="Functional Testing">
    Validate all MVP features against clear, testable acceptance criteria using Given-When-Then format
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Performance Testing">
    Conduct load and stress testing to confirm scalability under peak usage
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Security Testing">
    Perform penetration testing, vulnerability scanning, and compliance verification
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Usability Testing">
    Test with representative personas including users with low digital literacy
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Accessibility Testing">
    Evaluate using screen readers (JAWS, NVDA), keyboard-only navigation, and WCAG 2.1 AA audits
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="User Acceptance Testing (UAT)">
    Engage early adopters and target-persona users to validate real-world usability
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="CI/CD">
    Use automated testing pipelines for rapid iteration and reliable deployment
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
