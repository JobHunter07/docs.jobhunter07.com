# User Guides

Welcome to JobHunter07! These guides will help you make the most of the platform, whether you're actively job hunting, building your career, or supporting the community.

---

## Getting Started

### Create Your Account

1. **Sign Up**: Visit [jobhunter07.com](https://jobhunter07.com) and click "Sign Up"
2. **Choose Method**: Sign up with email or OAuth (Google, GitHub, LinkedIn)
3. **Verify Email**: Check your inbox and confirm your email address
4. **Complete Profile**: Add your skills, experience, and job preferences

### Set Up Your Profile

Your profile is your foundation on JobHunter07:

- **Basic Info**: Name, location, contact preferences
- **Skills**: Technologies, tools, domains of expertise
- **Experience**: Work history, projects, achievements
- **Job Preferences**: Roles, industries, locations, work arrangements
- **Privacy Settings**: Control who sees what information

**Pro Tip**: Your profile is private by default. You choose what to share and with whom.

## Core Features

### Job Application Tracker

Never lose track of a job application again.

#### Add an Application

1. Click "New Application" from dashboard
2. Enter job details:
   - Company name
   - Position title
   - Job posting URL
   - Application date
3. Set status (Applied, Interview, Offer, etc.)
4. Add notes, contacts, deadlines

#### Organize Applications

- **Board View**: Kanban-style columns by status
- **List View**: Sortable table of all applications
- **Calendar View**: See upcoming interviews and deadlines
- **Tags**: Create custom tags for organization

#### Track Progress

- Update status as applications progress
- Log interview rounds and feedback
- Set reminders for follow-ups
- Save correspondence and documents

#### Analytics

- Application response rates
- Time in each stage
- Success patterns
- Company/industry insights

### Community Support

Connect with fellow job seekers for mutual support.

#### Discussion Forums

- **Job Search Strategy**: Tips and techniques
- **Resume Reviews**: Get feedback on your resume
- **Interview Prep**: Practice and advice
- **Industry Chat**: Discuss specific fields
- **Wins & Support**: Celebrate success, find encouragement

#### Peer Review

- Request resume or portfolio reviews
- Give feedback to others (earn karma!)
- Get help with cover letters
- Practice interview questions

#### Accountability Partners

- Find a job search buddy
- Set shared goals
- Regular check-ins
- Mutual motivation

### Referral Network

Give and receive job referrals within the community.

#### Request a Referral

1. See a job at a company where community members work
2. Post a referral request with job details
3. Community members at that company can respond
4. Connect and coordinate the referral

#### Give Referrals

1. Share open positions at your company
2. Respond to referral requests
3. Earn goodwill and governance tokens
4. Build your network

**Etiquette**: Only request referrals for roles you're genuinely qualified for. Always follow up and be respectful of referrers' time.

### Job Recommendations

Get personalized job suggestions based on your profile and activity.

#### How It Works

Our recommendation engine considers:
- Your skills and experience
- Job preferences and history
- Application patterns
- Success indicators
- Community-validated job quality

#### Improve Recommendations

- Keep profile updated
- Mark jobs as relevant/not relevant
- Track applications consistently
- Provide feedback on suggestions

### Company Research

Learn about potential employers before applying.

#### Company Profiles

- **Overview**: Industry, size, funding, mission
- **Culture Insights**: Community-shared experiences
- **Interview Process**: What to expect, timelines
- **Salary Data**: Community-contributed ranges
- **Red Flags**: Warning signs from past applicants

#### Contribute Data

Help others by sharing:
- Interview experiences
- Salary information (anonymized)
- Company culture insights
- Hiring process details

## Privacy & Security

### Control Your Data

#### Privacy Levels

- **Private**: Only you see information
- **Community**: Other JobHunter07 users can see
- **Public**: Visible to employers and internet
- **Custom**: Granular control per field

#### Employer Visibility

Choose what employers can access:
- Searchable profile (opt-in)
- Specific profile sections
- Application history (always private)
- Community activity (configurable)

### Data Export & Deletion

- **Export**: Download all your data anytime (JSON, CSV)
- **Delete Account**: Permanently remove all data
- **Archive**: Pause account without deletion

## Tips for Success

### Job Application Best Practices

1. **Quality Over Quantity**: Apply to roles where you're a strong fit
2. **Customize Everything**: Tailor resume and cover letter per application
3. **Follow Up**: Send thank-you notes, check in appropriately
4. **Track Everything**: Use the tracker to stay organized
5. **Learn & Iterate**: Analyze what's working, adjust strategy

### Networking in the Community

1. **Give First**: Help others before asking for help
2. **Be Genuine**: Authentic connections are most valuable
3. **Stay Active**: Regular participation builds relationships
4. **Pay It Forward**: When you land a job, help others
5. **Respect Boundaries**: Be professional and considerate

### Platform Etiquette

- **Be Respectful**: Constructive criticism, no personal attacks
- **Stay On Topic**: Keep discussions relevant
- **No Spam**: Don't promote unrelated products/services
- **Protect Privacy**: Don't share others' information
- **Report Issues**: Flag inappropriate content or bugs

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Can't Log In

- Reset password via email
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try different browser or incognito mode
- Contact support if issues persist

#### Application Not Saving

- Check internet connection
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Try refreshing and re-entering
- Report bug if problem continues

#### Not Receiving Notifications

- Check email spam folder
- Verify notification settings in account
- Whitelist notifications@jobhunter07.com
- Update notification preferences
