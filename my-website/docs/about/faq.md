# Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about JobHunter07.

---

## General Questions

### What is JobHunter07?

JobHunter07 is a community-owned employment platform that puts workers first. Unlike traditional job boards, we're governed by a DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization), meaning our users collectively own and control the platform.

### How is JobHunter07 different from LinkedIn or Indeed?

- **Ownership**: Our community owns the platform through the DAO
- **Data**: You control your dataâ€”we don't sell it to third parties
- **Transparency**: Our code is open source, and governance is public
- **Community**: We prioritize mutual support over competition
- **Business Model**: We serve users, not advertisers

### Is JobHunter07 free to use?

Yes, core features are free for job seekers. We're exploring sustainable revenue models that don't exploit user data, including optional premium features and employer services.

### Do I need cryptocurrency to use JobHunter07?

No. While we use a token system for governance and incentives, you can use the core job-seeking features without any crypto knowledge or wallet.

## Privacy & Data

### What data do you collect?

We only collect data necessary to provide our services: profile information you choose to share, job search activity, and platform usage. See our [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy-policy) for full details.

### Who can see my data?

Only you control who sees your data. You decide what information to include in your profile, which employers to share it with, and how visible you are on the platform.

### Can I delete my account and data?

Yes, absolutely. You can export or delete your data at any time. We comply with data protection regulations and respect your right to be forgotten.

### Do you sell user data?

**No, never.** Selling user data violates our core values. We will never monetize your personal information.

## Community & Governance

### What is a DAO?

A DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization) is a community-governed organization where members vote on decisions. In JobHunter07, active participants can vote on features, policies, and how resources are allocated.

### How do I participate in governance?

1. Create an account and become an active community member
2. Earn governance tokens through participation
3. Vote on proposals in the governance portal
4. Submit your own proposals for community consideration

See our [DAO Overview](/governance/dao-overview) for more details.

### Do I have to participate in governance?

No. You can use JobHunter07 purely as a job-seeking tool. Governance participation is optional but encouraged.

## Features & Functionality

### What features does JobHunter07 offer?

- **Job Tracker**: Organize and track all your applications
- **Community Support**: Connect with peers, get resume reviews, and share strategies
- **Referrals**: Give and receive referrals within the community
- **Proactive Intelligence**: Get personalized job recommendations and market insights
- **Transparency Tools**: Research companies and sharing salary information

### Can employers post jobs on JobHunter07?

Yes, but with a difference. Employers who post must commit to transparency, fair hiring practices, and respect for candidate privacy. We're building a platform that serves workers first.

### Does JobHunter07 work internationally?

We're starting with a focus on specific markets but plan to expand globally. Check our roadmap for expansion plans.

## Getting Started

### How do I create an account?

Visit [JobHunter07](https://jobhunter07.com) and click "Sign Up." We'll guide you through creating a profile and setting your preferences.

### What should I include in my profile?

Include whatever you're comfortable sharing. At minimum, we recommend:
- Skills and experience
- Job preferences (location, role type, industries)
- How you'd like to participate in the community

### How do I get help if I'm stuck?

- Check our [User Guides](/users/guides)
- Ask in the community forums
- Contact our support team
- Join community office hours

## Business & Sustainability

### How does JobHunter07 make money?

We're exploring sustainable revenue models that align with our values:
- Optional premium features for power users
- Employer services (job postings, hiring tools)
- Grants and partnerships
- Community-approved revenue streams

Revenue models are decided through DAO governance.

### Is JobHunter07 a nonprofit?

We're structured as a for-profit entity with DAO governance, ensuring the community benefits from value creation. Specific legal structure may vary by jurisdiction.

### What happens to profits?

Profits are allocated based on DAO decisions, typically directed toward:
- Platform development and maintenance
- Community treasury
- Contributor compensation
- Ecosystem growth
